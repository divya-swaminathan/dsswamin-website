Billing systems rarely fail with a single obvious exception. More often, **configuration drift**, **partial data**, or **cross-system skew** compounds until finance or customers surface the problem days or weeks later.

I’m writing a longer piece on patterns that help: **proactive validation** before money moves, **dashboards that operators actually use**, and **alarms that separate noise from revenue risk**. Check back — or email **hello@divyaswaminathan.com** if you want to compare notes.
